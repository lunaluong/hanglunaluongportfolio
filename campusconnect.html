What is Campus Connect?

This project optimizes the use of social media for communication among college students, focusing on the experiences of international students in global institutions, particularly in China.

How was Campus Connect made?

Stemming from our experience as international students at Duke Kunshan University with an observation of a segregation between international and domestic students, Campus Connect aims to enhance the overall experience of students and facilitate their transition into a new educational and cultural environment by building a unified communication system that unifies all groups of students, allowing them to utilize the necessary functions for optimal promotion and interaction.
