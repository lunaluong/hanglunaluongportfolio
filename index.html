About SEVENTEEN - my idol

SEVENTEEN is a South Korean boy group known for their dynamic performances, self-produced music, and strong teamwork. Debuting in 2015 under Pledis Entertainment, the group consists of 13 members divided into three sub-units: hip-hop, vocal, and performance, showcasing diverse talents and musical versatility.

Renowned for hits like "Very Nice" and "Don't Wanna Cry," SEVENTEEN has gained a global fanbase, impressing with their intricate choreography and active involvement in songwriting and production, making them a standout force in K-pop.

About Virgo - my chart sign

Virgo, the sixth sign of the zodiac, is symbolized by the Maiden and is known for its analytical, practical, and detail-oriented nature. People born under this sign, which spans from August 23 to September 22, are often recognized for their dedication to helping others, strong work ethic, and thoughtful approach to life.
