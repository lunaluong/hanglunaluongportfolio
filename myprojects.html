Short film: The Red Sock

Besides music video, I also had an attempt with film making. I worked in a team of four students to make this short film for the #ShotonIphone Challenge. The theme of the film is the homesickness during Lunar New Year of two characters - a Cuban proxy trying to get away from a Vietnamese detective working in China. The film was filmed entirely on Iphone and edited with Da Vinci and Premiere Pro. I participated in scripting, shooting, and editing of this film.
